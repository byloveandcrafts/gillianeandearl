Questions & Answers

Things to Know

✦
Can guests take photos during the ceremony?
Guests are welcome to take photos anytime! We only ask that you be respectful during the ceremony. Our photographer will also be capturing all the beautiful moments throughout the day.
Where is the ceremony held?
The holy matrimony will be held at Nuestra Señora de la Annunciata Parish, Sitio Old (Lower) BosoBoso, Brgy. San Jose, Antipolo City 1870 Rizal, starting at 1:00 PM.
Where is the reception?
The reception will be at Fernwood Gardens Antipolo, Merano St. (formerly Ahon St.), Sitio San Lorenzo Ruiz, Brgy. San Roque, Antipolo City. Cocktail hour begins at 4:00 PM, followed by reception and dinner at 5:00 PM, and an after party at 9:00 PM.
What is the dress code?
We kindly request guests to wear Burgundy, Metallic Gold, Charcoal Gray, or similar formal tones. White and ivory are lovingly reserved for the bride. Denim is not encouraged to keep the elegance of our celebration.
When is the RSVP deadline?
Please kindly RSVP by September 1, 2026. This helps us ensure that every detail of our celebration is perfectly prepared for all our beloved guests.
