Questions & Answers

Things to Know

✦
Can guests take photos during the ceremony?
Guests are welcome to take photos anytime! We only ask that you be respectful during the ceremony. Our photographer will also be capturing all the beautiful moments throughout the day.
Where is the ceremony held?
The holy matrimony will be held at Nuestra Señora de la Annunciata Parish, Sitio Old (Lower) Boso Boso, Brgy. San Jose, Antipolo City 1870 Rizal, starting at 1:00 PM.
Where is the reception?
The reception will be at Fernwood Gardens Antipolo, Merano St. (formerly Ahon St.), Sitio San Lorenzo Ruiz, Brgy. San Roque, Antipolo City. Cocktail hour begins at 4:00 PM, followed by reception and dinner at 5:00 PM, and an after party at 9:00 PM.
What is the dress code?
We kindly request guests to wear Champagne, Tan, Terracotta, Mauve, Taupe Gray, or Toffee Brown tones. White and ivory are lovingly reserved for the bride. Denim is not encouraged to keep the elegance of our celebration.
When is the RSVP deadline?
Please kindly RSVP by September 1, 2026. This helps us ensure that every detail of our celebration is perfectly prepared for all our beloved guests.
Can I bring a plus-one?
If your invitation includes a plus-one, it will be reflected on your RSVP. Thank you for understanding our limited seating.
Can I bring my kids?
We love your little ones dearly, but our wedding will be an adults-only affair unless otherwise stated on your invitation. Thank you for understanding and celebrating with us.
Should I fill out the RSVP if I can't attend?
We'll truly miss celebrating with you. If you can't make it, you may simply let us know directly instead of filling out the RSVP form.
Can I make changes after I send it?
Plans change — we understand! Simply reach out to us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate updates.
